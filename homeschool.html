Home School Consulting

Led by our knowledgeable consultant who has over 17 years experience in home education (grades Kindergarten through high school), our informative, interactive workshops focus on how to maneuver the challenges of home schooling.

Are you overwhelmed and stressed out by homeschooling?
JeltsTriangle is here to help.

We offer carefully considered solutions to questions such as:

- What is the most effective way to teach Preschool - Kindergarten children?

- I am struggling with time management. Can you help?

- What are the alternatives to online classes?

- Do you have scheduling advice for those teaching multiple children?

All these concerns and more are addressed at our customized, interactive workshops, which require a minimum of 10 participants.
